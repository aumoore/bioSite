From The Weeknd to ...

In an interview recently (May 2023) with The Weeknd, he mentioned he future plans as wanting to kill off his stage name The Weeknd. He stated he's "ready to close The Weeknd chapter" and plans on making one last album under his alter ego. He still plans on making music, but hinted that maybe it will be under Abel, or maybe it could be as The Weeknd, but will still keep moving forward to closing The Weeknd. He also said that he's said everything he can say as The Weeknd, which a main reason why he's heading in this direction.
